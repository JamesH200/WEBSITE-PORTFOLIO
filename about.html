About

Embarking on a creative journey at mulitple universities, I immersed myself in graphic design and business for three years, although the formal graduation didn't come to pass. Shifting gears in 2012, I found myself behind the wheel of trucks, delving into logistics, and acquiring a hands-on education in the industry. The culmination of my experiences led to the inception of my own logistics company in 2019, where I've elevated our services to a premium tier. Balancing the demands of entrepreneurship, I find solace and joy in the company of my three children, fostering a deep love for family time. Beyond the boardroom, my passion extends to creating connections that positively impact the world, complemented by a zest for global exploration with at least one new country visited each year. Nestled in the scenic landscapes of California, my appreciation for the outdoors finds expression in my love for camping, hiking, and various outdoor pursuits. Embracing the ever-evolving tech landscape, I've recently ventured into coding for web design, marking the beginning of a new chapter where creativity meets innovation. Thriving on connections and creation, I am fueled by a genuine love for people and a commitment to making a difference in both the business and natural realms.
